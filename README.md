# Supply Chain Risk Analytics & Late Delivery Prediction

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing global supply chain data to identify operational risks and improve delivery performance. The goal is to understand product demand patterns, evaluate logistics efficiency, and detect delivery delays using data analytics and machine learning techniques.

By combining data analysis with an interactive dashboard, the project provides insights into supply chain operations and helps businesses monitor performance across different regions, product categories, and shipping modes.

---

## Business Problem

Modern supply chains generate large volumes of operational data, but many organizations struggle to extract meaningful insights from this data.

Issues such as delayed deliveries, inefficient shipping modes, and inconsistent product demand can lead to increased operational costs and poor customer satisfaction.

Without a proper monitoring system, businesses may find it difficult to identify problem areas in logistics operations and respond quickly to potential disruptions.

This project aims to analyze supply chain data to detect delivery delays, understand product demand, and highlight operational inefficiencies.

---

## Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

* Analyze supply chain transaction data to understand sales and product demand patterns
* Identify regions with higher delivery delays
* Evaluate the performance of different shipping modes
* Predict late delivery risk using machine learning models
* Develop an interactive dashboard to monitor supply chain performance

## Key Insights

* Central America and Western Europe show the highest number of delivery delays.
* Product demand is highest for categories such as Cleats and Women's Apparel.
* Different shipping modes significantly impact delivery time and operational efficiency.
* Sales performance varies across global markets, with some regions contributing more to overall revenue.

The combination of data analytics, machine learning, and dashboard visualization provides a comprehensive view of supply chain operations. These insights can help organizations monitor delivery performance, identify problem areas, and make more informed business decisions.
